# Releasing Flama 1.11

We're pleased to announce the release of **[Flama 1.11.0](https://github.com/vortico/flama/releases/tag/v1.11.0/)**! 🎉
This smaller, focused release packs a punch, introducing a significant performance boost that will benefit every single
request your Flama application handles.

The core highlight of this version is a crucial internal improvement focused entirely on speed and efficiency.

- **Cache for Component Resolution Value** (1.11.0): Say hello to faster requests! We've implemented a **cache for
  component resolution values**. This means that the framework now avoids redundant lookups and computations during
  request processing, leading to a **drastic increase in framework performance** on every incoming request. This
  optimization makes your APIs snappier and more scalable right out of the box!

While this release doesn't introduce major new user-facing features, the performance gains from this caching mechanism
are substantial and impact the core responsiveness of your services.

We look forward to continuing to build on this foundation in upcoming releases!

Stay tuned for more, and happy coding! 🚀
